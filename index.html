About Me


I am a recently certified Full-Stack Web Developer seeking to utilize my strong problem solving and leadership skills to help individuals, companies and clients optimize their web applications and data processes.

After graduating from the University of Austin in 2006 with a bachelor’s in Biology, I had the opportunity to utilize my natural, quick learning ability and strong leadership skills in the oil and gas service industry. In the last twelve years I have held positions as a laboratory technician, project manager, field specialist, laboratory supervisor and technical advisor. I have also helped develop training course and enjoyed being a mentor and teacher to new employees. As a supervisor, I’ve had the opportunity to work with a great team and together we maximized project efficiency and maintained great client communication. This effort in turn led to exceeding our revenue goals, retaining customers and presenting our lab as a reputable fluid analysis provider.

I have a strong desire to be challenged daily and in helping others in my professional career. With my broad professional experience, strong drive and work ethic, and love for web development, I have no doubt of being an immediate asset for an employer willing to provide the opportunity to showcase my skills.
